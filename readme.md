# Unlimited Innovation — Landing Page Redesign

A high-impact, single-page landing page with an **Obsidian Glass** aesthetic (`#050505` black + `#FF7E00` orange), built in React with SEO-optimized semantic HTML.

---

## Sections (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Navigation — Fixed "Command Bar" Header

- Sticky top bar with company name left, smooth-scroll nav links right (About, Services, Partners, Clients, Contact)
- Semi-transparent dark glass background with orange accent on active/hover

### 2. Hero — Full Viewport

- Left-aligned bold headline: **"Engineering the Unthinkable."**
- Tagline about being a trusted digital transformation partner
- Two CTA buttons: "Start a Project" (orange filled) and "View Work" (bordered)
- Right side: animated technical artifact (code snippet or geometric pattern)
- Framer Motion entrance animation

### 3. About — Mission & Vision

- Two glass cards side by side with orange-tinted borders
- Mission and Vision content from the original site, refined copy
- Geometric icons with orange accent color

### 4. Services — Bento Grid

- 6 service cards in a responsive 3-column grid:
  - Platform & Web Development
  - AI & Data Science
  - Big Data Solutions
  - Digital Transformation
  - Business Intelligence
  - E-Commerce Solutions
- Each card: glass material, orange icon, hover effect with orange border glow
- Hover reveals tech stack tags (React, Node, AWS, etc.)

### 5. Tech Stack Showcase _(new)_

- Horizontal display of technology logos/icons the company works with
- Subtle animation (scroll or fade)

### 6. Process / How We Work _(new)_

- 4-step visual timeline: Discovery → Design → Develop → Deploy
- Clean numbered steps with brief descriptions
- Orange accent line connecting the steps

### 7. Key Metrics _(new)_

- Animated counters: "99.9% Uptime", "50+ Projects", "15+ Partners", etc.
- Dark cards with large mono-font numbers in orange

### 8. Partners

- Logo grid of partner companies (placeholder logos for now)
- Glass card container

### 9. Clients / Testimonials _(new)_

- Client logo strip + 2-3 testimonial cards with quotes
- Placeholder content

### 10. Contact — High-Conversion Form

- Left: contact info + tagline
- Right: visual-only form (Name, Email, Subject, Message, Submit button)
- Submit button with orange fill and hover animation

### 11. Footer

- Company info, quick links, social icons
- "© Unlimited Innovation" with orange accent

## Design System

- **Background**: `#050505` with `#0A0A0A` cards
- **Accent**: `#FF7E00` (orange) for icons, borders, CTAs
- **Text**: `#F5F5F5` primary, `#999` secondary
- **Borders**: `1px solid rgba(255, 126, 0, 0.15)`
- **Corners**: Sharp (`rounded-sm` or none)
- **Font**: Geist Sans (via Google Fonts) + monospace for labels
- **Motion**: Framer Motion with smooth entrance animations

## SEO

- Semantic HTML (`<section>`, `<header>`, `<footer>`, `<main>`)
- Meta tags, Open Graph tags, structured data
- Descriptive `alt` text and heading hierarchy
